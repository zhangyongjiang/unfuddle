One Click Shortcut

for quick access of your favorite applications!

 

Use Case 1 - One Click!

One click to launch your favorite application.
  1. Open One Click Shortcut application
  2. Add shortcut. Find your favorite application, click it. The application icon will appear on the right side under .
  3. Press BACK button to exit One Click Shortcut.
  4. Click on the top right corner will launch your application immediately.
* Please make sure you have one and only one shortcut defined.

Use Case 2 - Multiple shortcuts

  1. Open One Click Shortcut application
  2. Find your favorite application, click it. The application icon will appear on the right side under .
  3. Repeat step above to add more shortcuts. To remove a shortcut, click the shortcut icon on the right side, click to remove it
  4. Press BACK button to exit One Click Shortcut when you are done.
  5. Click on the top right corner will list all shortcuts you added, click one to launch the application.

Use Case 3 - Quick switching between running applications

Same as use case 2. If you have defined shortcuts for current running applications, you can use One Click Shortcut for quick switching.

Tips

If you have no shortcut defined, click the will launch the One Click Shortcut application itself.

Long touching icon will bring you to One Click Shortcut application.
